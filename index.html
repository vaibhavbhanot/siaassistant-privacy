SiaAssistant Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2025

Simple Summary: SiaAssistant does not collect, store, or share any of your personal information. Your privacy is 100% protected.

1. Information We Collect

None. SiaAssistant does not collect any personal information, usage data, or analytics from users. The app operates entirely on your device with no data transmission.

2. How We Use Your Information

Since we don't collect any information, we don't use any information. Everything you do in the app stays on your device.

3. Data Storage

SiaAssistant does not store any user data on external servers. All app functionality occurs locally on your device. We do not use cloud storage or remote databases.

4. Third-Party Services

SiaAssistant does not integrate with any third-party services, analytics platforms, or advertising networks. No data is shared with third parties because no data is collected.

5. Internet Connection

SiaAssistant works completely offline and does not require an internet connection. The app does not communicate with any external servers or services.

6. Children's Privacy

SiaAssistant is safe for users of all ages, including children under 13. Since we collect no data, there is no risk to children's privacy. Parents can use this app with confidence knowing their child's privacy is fully protected.

7. Text-to-Speech

The text-to-speech functionality in SiaAssistant uses your device's built-in iOS speech synthesis. This is handled entirely by iOS and no speech data is recorded, stored, or transmitted by SiaAssistant.

8. Changes to This Policy

If we ever change our privacy practices (which would only happen if we added features that required data collection), we will update this policy and notify users through an app update description.

9. Your Rights

Since we don't collect any data, there is no data to access, modify, or delete. You have complete privacy by design.
